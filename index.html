Getting Connected to the ILIA Intelligence Hub

Two phases — Phase 1 is available now. Phase 2 coming as the CIH Portal is built out.

Phase 1 Active · Phase 2 In Development
PHASE 1 Simplicity Claude Account — Shared ILIA Project ✓ Available Now
1
Log Into Your Simplicity Claude Account
Sign in at claude.ai using your Simplicity Group work email. Make sure you're on the Simplicity Group · Team plan — visible at the bottom left of the screen.
→ Go to claude.ai
2
Open the ILIA Central Intelligence Project
Click Projects in the left sidebar → click the Shared with you tab → open ILIA Central Intelligence. This is the shared project with full ILIA platform context, GitHub access, JIRA, Confluence, and all connectors pre-configured.
→ Open Claude Projects
3
Enable Your Connectors
In Claude Settings → Connectors, connect your personal tools. The shared project tools (JIRA, Confluence, GitHub) are already configured — you just need your own Gmail, Drive, and Calendar.
📧 Gmail 📁 Google Drive 📅 Calendar
4
Install Claude in Chrome (Recommended)
Install the Claude Chrome extension to enable browser automation — Claude can navigate pages, click buttons, and assist with ILIA and other web-based workflows directly.
→ Chrome Web Store
5
Start Working — You're Connected
Open any chat inside the ILIA Central Intelligence project and start working. Claude already knows the full ILIA platform, your team, active projects, and all connected tools. Example prompts to get started:
"Create a new prototype called ilia-[name] and push it to the NIW GitHub org"
"What's the current status of the DBR project and are there any open blockers?"
"Create a JIRA ticket for [feature] with acceptance criteria"
PHASE 2 ILIA CIH Portal — Role-Configured Web Stations 🏗️ In Development
6
Get Your CIH Portal Invite
When the ILIA CIH Portal launches, each employee will receive an invite to their role-configured workspace. Your seat type (Operations, Support, Sales, Marketing, Dev, Admin) determines your personalized dashboard and tools.
○ View Portal Vision → → CIH Hub Prototype
7
Log Into Your Role Dashboard
A branded NIW web app — your role-specific dashboard with AI chat, connected tools, and panels built for your function. Operations sees JIRA queues and SOPs. Support sees client flows. Marketing sees asset library and campaign tools. No Claude.ai needed.
○ Coming Soon
8
Work From Your Station
Phase 2 replaces the need to work inside Claude.ai directly. Your CIH Portal station is your daily workspace — all intelligence, all tools, all context — in one NIW-owned platform purpose-built for your role.
Operations Support Sales Marketing Dev / PM Admin
